Strategic Direction:
Vision, Mission, Objectives & Execution

战略方向：愿景、使命、目标与执行
2.1

The Five-Stage Strategic Framework 五阶段战略框架

#StageDescription
1Vision, Mission & ValuesDefine where you're going, why you exist, and what you believe 定义去向、存在理由和信念
2Set ObjectivesFinancial + strategic objectives, measurable and time-bound 设定可衡量的财务和战略目标
3Craft the StrategyChoose how to achieve those objectives 制定实现目标的战略
4Execute the StrategyImplement through people, structure, and systems 通过人员、结构、系统执行
5Monitor, Evaluate & AdjustExternal and internal changes require ongoing adaptation 持续监控和调整

战略过程包含五个阶段：制定愿景和使命 → 设定目标 → 制定战略 → 执行战略 → 监督和调整。这不是一个线性流程，外部环境变化和内部情况都可能迫使你随时回头调整。

2.2

Vision, Mission & Values 愿景、使命与价值观

Vision Statement 愿景声明

The Vision answers 'Where are we going?' — future-oriented, inspirational, and specific enough to enable self-alignment.

AttributeWhy It Matters
Future-orientedStarts with 'We aspire to be...' — not about past 面向未来，不谈过去
The What AND the WhyPeople need to understand the compelling reason behind the direction 要回答'是什么'和'为什么'
Distinctive & inspiringUses language that sets the company apart; motivates behavior change 有特色、有感染力
Clear & specific (not vague)Vagueness → inaction; people can't self-align without a clear picture 模糊的愿景导致无法行动
Feasible & memorableMust ask people to do realistic things; short enough to recall 切实可行且好记
Not generic superlativesAvoid empty phrases like 'the preeminent provider of...' 避免空洞的夸张措辞

愿景声明要面向未来，要回答「我们想去哪里」以及「为什么这很重要」。太模糊的愿景没有价值——员工不知道自己该怎么做。好的愿景让整个生态系统中的合作伙伴和利益相关者都能对齐。

Mission Statement 使命声明

The Mission answers 'Why do we exist?' Making money is assumed for a for-profit company — NOT the mission. The mission is about what you do so well that it generates profit as a byproduct.

Should include: product/service, needs satisfied, customer groups, differentiating element.

使命声明回答的是「我们为什么存在」。赚钱是一个营利性公司的前提，不是使命本身。如果你把某件事做好，利润自然会来。
Example

TOMS Shoes

For every pair purchased, a pair donated to a child in the developing world. Values-driven mission that transcends profit.

Core Values 核心价值观

Values sit between mission and vision — intentionally. If the values held by most employees are NOT aligned with the direction the company wants to go, there will be significant friction and failure.

价值观处于使命和愿景之间，原因在于：如果大多数员工的价值观与公司想去的方向不一致，就会遇到阻力甚至失败。谷歌就是个例子——一些工程师因为个人价值观拒绝参与军事AI项目。
2.3

Setting Objectives 设定目标

LetterMeaning中文
SSpecific — Clear, not vague明确具体
MMeasurable — Can be tracked可以衡量
AAchievable — Realistic切实可行
RRelevant — Connected to strategy与战略相关
TTime-bound — Has a deadline有时间限制

Need both Financial Objectives (lagging indicators — outcomes) and Strategic Objectives (leading indicators — drivers of outcomes). Both short-term (annual) and long-term objectives required.

WARNING — Stretch Targets: Can motivate better performance but must be carefully designed. Wells Fargo case: staff incentivized to open new accounts ended up opening fraudulent ones to hit their numbers.

目标必须是SMART的。财务目标是「滞后指标」——是结果，把其他事情做好之后才会出现的。战略目标才是驱动因素。拉伸目标听起来很好，但如果激励机制设计不当，会导致不道德行为，富国银行（Wells Fargo）就是例子——员工为了完成开户指标开了大量假账户。

2.4

Balanced Scorecard 平衡计分卡

Developed by Kaplan & Norton (1992). Framework: set objectives, measures, targets, and initiatives across four perspectives:

#PerspectiveKey Question
1CustomerWhat do we need to do well for the customer? 我们要为客户做好什么？
2Internal ProcessesWhat processes/technology must change to deliver that value? 要改变哪些流程和技术？
3Learning & Growth (People)Do we have the right people, structure, training, incentives? 有没有合适的人才和组织？
4FinancialIf we succeed at 1–3, financial results will follow 做好前三项，财务结果自然跟来

平衡计分卡1992年由卡普兰和诺顿提出。四个维度：客户、内部流程、学习与成长（人才）、财务。顺序很重要：先确定要为客户做什么，然后倒推需要改变哪些流程和技术，再看需要什么样的人才和组织结构，最后财务结果自然跟来。大多数管理者容易陷入只看财务数字的误区，但真正重要的是那些非财务指标。

2.5

Strategy Levels 战略层级

LevelFocus / Example
Corporate StrategyPortfolio of businesses: hold, grow, reduce, or exit 管理业务组合，类似投资组合
Business StrategyCompetitive positioning within a specific business unit 单个业务单元的竞争策略
Functional StrategyDepartment-level plans (R&D, Marketing, HR, Finance) 各部门的计划
Operating StrategyPlant/team-level execution plans 工厂或团队层面的执行计划

All four levels must be aligned. Once strategic fit is created across all levels, the strategy becomes more inimitable — harder for competitors to copy.

战略分四个层级，这四层必须相互对齐。当所有层级都相互配合、形成战略契合（strategic fit）时，战略就更难被竞争对手复制了。

2.6

Strategy Execution 战略执行

Execution is the most difficult and most important part of strategy. A weak strategy executed well can still succeed; a great strategy executed poorly will fail.
  • Structure — most common failure: executing new strategy under a structure designed for the OLD strategy 结构——最常见的失败原因：用旧策略的组织架构去执行新策略
  • Right Skills, People & Resources — foundational requirement 合适的人才、技能和资源是基础
  • Policies & Procedures — review and revise what no longer fits the new strategy 政策和程序——审查和修订不再适合新战略的内容
  • Information & Operating Systems — enable task execution, monitoring, early warning 信息和运营系统——支持任务执行、监控、提供预警
  • Culture — healthy, collaborative; a culture of fear or disruption will undermine even the best strategy 文化——健康合作的文化；充满恐惧或内耗的文化会摧毁任何战略
  • Leadership — inspire and influence; get people genuinely on board 领导力——鼓舞和影响人，而不只是让人服从
  • Accountability — hold teams and individuals to their committed targets 问责——对承诺的目标负责

执行是战略中最重要也最难的部分。即使战略不完美，执行好了也能成功；但战略再好，执行差了也会失败。执行失败最常见的原因之一是：用旧策略的组织架构去执行新策略。

2.7

The Board's Role in Strategy 董事会在战略中的角色

The board does NOT formulate strategy — that is the CEO and leadership team's job.
  • Stress-test the strategy — ask penetrating questions; verify SMART objectives and organizational capabilities 对战略进行「压力测试」——提出尖锐问题，验证目标和执行能力
  • Ensure alignment with mission and values — if CEO is going in the wrong direction, the board must act (including replacing the CEO) 确保与使命和价值观对齐——必要时更换CEO
  • Raise leadership caliber — the bar for both senior executives and board directors has risen significantly 提高领导层质量标准——过去15-20年标准大幅提高
  • Compensation alignment — executive pay must align with the RIGHT performance outcomes 薪酬对齐——高管薪酬必须与正确的绩效结果挂钩
  • Monitor conduct — if behavior is inappropriate, unethical or illegal, the board is responsible 监督行为——不当行为未处理，董事会可能被追责

董事会不制定战略，那是CEO和管理层的工作。但董事会要对战略进行「压力测试」——提出尖锐问题，确认目标是SMART的，判断管理层是否有能力执行。大众汽车（Volkswagen）是一个典型的反面案例——因为董事会缺乏独立性，使得排放造假丑闻得以发生且长期未被发现。

Case Study

Volkswagen — Governance Failure

The lack of board independence enabled major scandals (emissions fraud) to go unchecked. A board that is too close to management — or lacks truly independent voices — cannot perform its oversight role effectively.

Lesson: Board independence is not a formality — it is the structural guarantee that protects the company from management overreach and ethical drift.

Case Study

Maple Leaf Foods — Sustainability Leadership

A prominent Canadian example of a company that integrated sustainability into its core strategic direction. The board and CEO aligned on long-term ESG commitments as part of the strategic vision — not just a PR exercise.
