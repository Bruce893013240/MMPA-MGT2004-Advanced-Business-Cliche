International Strategy:
Competing in Global Markets

国际战略：在全球市场竞争
4.1

Why Companies Enter Foreign Markets 进入海外市场的原因

ReasonDetail
Gain new customersDomestic markets mature; growth expectations require international expansion 国内市场成熟后，公司增长依赖海外市场
Lower costsEconomies of scale, experience curve, increased purchasing power 规模经济、经验曲线、采购议价能力
Cheaper production inputsLabor, energy, raw materials significantly lower in certain countries 特定国家的劳动力、能源成本更低
Deploy core competenciesIf your capabilities don't exist in target market, you can grow quickly 将在国内成熟的核心能力带到海外空白市场
Access resourcesCritical for extraction/energy — you go where the resources are 提取类行业别无选择，必须去资源在的地方

公司进入海外市场最主要的驱动力是：国内市场逐渐成熟时需要寻找新的增长来源，同时利用更低廉的生产要素成本（劳动力、能源），把在国内已经成熟的核心能力带到还没有这种能力的海外市场，以及获取海外的自然资源。

4.2

Porter's Diamond of National Advantage 波特国家竞争优势钻石模型

Explains why certain industries thrive in certain countries. Though somewhat dated, still widely referenced.

Diamond ComponentDescription
Demand ConditionsLarge, sophisticated home demand forces scale and capability-building before going international 国内需求规模和成熟度塑造出口竞争力
Factor ConditionsNatural resources, climate, labor cost, energy, infrastructure — e.g., Ecuador: altitude + sunlight + low wages = world-class rose exports 生产要素条件（自然资源、劳动力成本等）
Firm Strategy, Structure & RivalryIntense domestic competition forces firms to become world-class 国内激烈竞争造就世界级企业
Related & Supporting IndustriesIndustry clusters enable growth — e.g., GTA auto + tool/die/mold makers; Mississauga pharma cluster 产业集群效应

Uses of the Diamond Model

  • Predict which foreign competitors may enter your market 预测哪些外国竞争对手最可能进入你的市场
  • Choose which markets to enter first 选择优先进入哪些市场
  • Decide where to locate different value chain activities 决定在哪里布局不同的价值链活动

波特的国家竞争优势钻石模型解释了为什么某些行业在某些国家特别强。四个维度：需求条件、要素条件、企业战略结构和竞争状态、以及相关支持性产业。厄瓜多尔是要素条件的好例子——高海拔、多日照、低劳动力成本，让它成为世界顶级玫瑰出口国。可以用这个模型预测哪些外国竞争对手最可能进入你的市场。

4.3

Exchange Rate Risk 汇率风险

Currencies move 24 hours a day across global financial markets (Toronto → Vancouver → Hong Kong → Frankfurt). Three types:

Transaction Risk 交易风险

Payment terms of 90–120 days — exchange rate may move adversely before payment received.

90-120天的付款期内汇率可能不利变动，导致实际收款缩水。
Translation Risk 折算风险

Consolidating multinational financials — all foreign currencies convert to reporting currency; gains or losses arise.

合并财务报表时外币折算产生损益。
Economic / Operating Risk 经济/运营风险

Plant in another country becomes exposed if fundamental market conditions shift adversely — a long-term structural exposure.

海外设厂后市场条件根本性变化导致的暴露，影响公司市场地位和竞争力。

Hedging tools: Forward contracts, currency swaps lock in exchange rates and reduce exposure.

汇率风险分三类：交易风险（90-120天付款期间汇率移动导致收款缩水）、折算风险（合并财务报表时外币折算产生损益）、以及经济风险（海外设厂后市场条件根本性变化导致的暴露）。可以用远期合约和货币掉期来对冲汇率风险。

4.4

Three Pre-Entry Questions 进入市场前的三个核心问题

Over 90% of even small and mid-sized North American businesses are involved in international trade. Before choosing HOW to enter, answer these three questions:

QuestionKey Considerations
1. Which market?GDP growth rate, disposable income, political stability, competitive landscape, cultural fit GDP增速、可支配收入、政治稳定性、文化适配性
2. When to enter?First mover (brand, switching costs, learning) vs. Late mover (avoid pioneer costs, leapfrog) 先发vs后发的权衡，与Session 3逻辑相同
3. At what scale?Small (lower risk, slower brand build) vs. Large (faster growth, stronger political leverage, bigger loss if it fails) 小规模试水vs大规模投入的风险收益权衡
Real-World Example

Domino's — Costly Cultural Assumptions

  • Japan: 'Pepperoni' was assumed to be a pepper (vegetable); took years of market education to become #1 pizza delivery brand
  • UK: 'One call does it all' failed because 'call' means a face-to-face visit in British English

进入海外市场之前要回答三个核心问题：进哪个市场（评估市场吸引力）、什么时候进（先发还是后发的权衡）、以什么规模进入（小规模试水vs大规模投入）。Domino's在日本和英国的案例是先发「开拓成本」的生动例子——英国人把'call'理解成「上门拜访」，日本人以为'pepperoni'是某种辣椒。

4.5

Five Primary Modes of Entry 五种主要进入模式

ModeKey ProsKey Cons
1. Exporting 出口 Low capital; uses existing capacity; no direct investment risk 低资本要求 Transportation costs, tariffs, exchange rate risk, less control
2. Licensing 许可 Fast; passive royalty income; low capital 快速、被动收入 Loss of control over technology, operational quality issues
3. Franchising 特许经营 Fast expansion without own capital; more control than licensing (can cancel) 快速扩张且资本要求低 Quality control across franchisees; cultural adaptation needed
4. FDI — Acquisition 收购 Fast; existing cash flow; avoids tariffs; market knowledge 快速、有现成现金流 Risk of overpaying; integration issues; culture clash
5. FDI — Greenfield 绿地投资 Transfer your full culture/tech; deep market learning 充分转移企业文化和技术 Capital intensive; political risk; slow to build
6. Joint Ventures / Alliances 合资/联盟 Local knowledge; shared risk; economies of scale 本地知识、风险分担 IP leakage risk; shared control issues

五种进入模式各有不同的控制程度、资本要求和风险特征。出口门槛最低但控制力最弱；FDI（直接投资）控制力最强但资本要求和风险最高。收购更快、有现成现金流，但文化整合难；绿地成本可能更低（如果没有合理价格的收购标的），但速度慢、风险大。收购的「市场份额派图」比喻：收购已有的一块市场份额比绿地进入引发的竞争报复要小得多。

4.6

Three International Strategic Approaches 三种国际战略方式

Plotted on a 2×2 matrix: X-axis = need for local responsiveness; Y-axis = benefits from global integration.

StrategyProsCons
Global Strategy 全球战略
Think globally, act globally		 Lower cost from economies of scale; consistent global brand; cross-market innovation 规模经济、全球品牌一致性 Difficult to address local needs; higher coordination costs
Multidomestic 多本土战略
Think locally, act locally		 Precise local customization; fast local response; target specific rivals 精确本地化定制、快速本地响应 Difficult to transfer capabilities; higher production costs; no scale
Transnational 跨国战略
Balance of both		 Hybrid — captures some global scale AND some local adaptation 兼顾全球规模效益和本地适应性 Most complex to manage; requires flexible coordination

三种国际战略模式：全球战略（全球标准化，成本低但本地适应性差）、多本土战略（每个市场独立运作，本地适应性强但失去规模效益）、跨国战略（两者的混合体，最难执行）。苹果用富士康在中国组装电脑——设计是高科技，但组装不是，放在低成本地区做是合理的。

4.7

Concentrate vs. Disperse Activities 集中还是分散

Concentrate when…Disperse when…
Manufacturing costs significantly lower in specific locations 特定地区的生产成本显著更低 Buyer-facing activities require physical proximity to customers 面向客户的活动需要物理上靠近客户
Significant economies of scale from running high volumes through one plant 单一工厂高产量带来显著规模经济 Transportation costs are prohibitive for centralized production 集中生产的运输成本过高
Superior natural resources are location-specific 独特自然资源具有地理专属性 Trade barriers make exporting from central location too expensive — use FDI instead 贸易壁垒让集中出口太贵，应做FDI绕过关税
Better coordination of related activities 相关活动的更好协调 Political risk warrants geographic diversification 政治风险需要地理多元化

什么时候应该集中生产？当某地生产成本显著更低、或有规模效益、或有独特自然资源的时候。什么时候应该分散？当运输成本过高、当贸易壁垒让集中出口变得太贵（此时应做FDI绕过关税）、或者当要分散政治风险和供应链中断风险的时候。

4.8

Impact of Government Policies 政府政策的影响

  • Tax incentives (e.g., Ireland's low corporate tax rate) — 税收优惠
  • Low cost loans — 低息贷款
  • Worker training programs — 员工培训
  • Environmental regulations — 环境法规
  • Subsidies to local competitors — 补贴本地竞争者
  • Import restrictions, tariffs and quotas — 进口限制、关税和配额
  • Local content requirements (e.g., Canadian auto industry) — 本地化要求
  • Regulatory approvals — 监管审批
  • Repatriation of profits — 利润汇回限制
  • Local partner ownership requirements — 本地合作伙伴所有权要求
4.9

Competing in Developing Countries 在发展中国家竞争

For Foreign Firms Entering Developing Markets 外来企业的进入策略

  • Compete on price — disposable income is lower; products must be priced accordingly 以价格竞争——当地可支配收入低，产品定价必须相应调整
  • Adapt your business model — infrastructure (roads, electricity) is unreliable; distribution must be redesigned 调整商业模式——基础设施（道路、电力）不可靠，产品设计和分销渠道必须重新设计
  • Avoid markets requiring ethical compromises (e.g., bribery) — WTO now penalizes bribery; Canadian GTA firms lost market access after bribery scandals 避免需要行贿才能运营的市场——WTO现有反贿赂规定，GTA公司曾因此被迫出售

For Local Firms Defending Against Global Giants 本地企业的防御策略

  • Exploit shortcomings in local infrastructure — locals navigate poor roads and informal networks better than multinationals 利用对本地基础设施缺陷的了解
  • Leverage deep local market knowledge and relationships — customize in ways that large foreign firms cannot 发挥深厚的本地市场知识和关系网络
  • Rapid acquisition strategy — acquire other local firms to become a much larger, harder-to-displace competitor 通过快速并购做大自己，在外来巨头完全站稳脚跟之前成为更难被打败的竞争对手

在发展中国家竞争，外来大公司要准备以更低价格参与竞争，并根据当地不完善的基础设施重新设计商业模式。有些市场需要行贿才能开展业务，但WTO现在有反贿赂规定，加拿大GTA地区就有公司因此被迫出售。本地企业面对外国巨头时的防御策略包括：利用对本地基础设施缺陷的了解、发挥深厚的本地关系网络、以及通过快速并购做大自己。
